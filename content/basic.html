What Are Oxidation and Reduction?

Oxidation

The complete or partial loss of electrons or gain of oxygen.

Reduction

The complete or partial gain of electrons or gain of oxygen.

Redox Reactions That Form Ions

During a reaction between a metal and a nonmetal, electrons are transferred from atoms of the metal to atoms of the nonmetal.

Redox With Covalent Compounds

With redox reactions involving covalent compounds, the there is not a complete transfer of electrons. Instead electrons are pulled more closely towards the more electronegative element, such as oxygen.

Corrosion

Oxidation of a metal by oxygen corrodes many metals including iron.
